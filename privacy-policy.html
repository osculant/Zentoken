Privacy Policy

How we protect your information at Zentoken

Zentoken LLC ("Zentoken", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in connection with our premium cryptocurrency consultancy services based in Miami, Florida.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number, financial information (portfolio size, investment objectives, risk tolerance), tax identification details, and any information you provide during consultations or onboarding.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage collected through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Deliver personalized investment advisory, portfolio management, security audits, and market intelligence services.
  • Communicate with you regarding consultations, reports, and market updates.
  • Comply with applicable U.S. laws and regulations (including AML/KYC requirements).

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Trusted service providers (custodians, tax software, analytics partners) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory authorities when required by law (SEC, IRS, FinCEN, etc.).

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data, particularly in relation to cryptocurrency assets.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. California residents have additional rights under the CCPA/CPRA.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to improve your experience on our website. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
